About

A people-oriented full stack developer with a passion for
teamwork, learning, and innovative design.

I've always pursued knowledge. Whether it has been health and fitness, playing music, or writing code to program computers. I soak up information and love putting it to use. This has caused my path so far to have many twists and turns. I've been a personal trainer, an aspiring musician, I planned to join the medical field and work as a physical therapist, but none of this provided my the happiness I sought after.

Programming computers for me is where I can dream and then create a product for the world to use. It has ended my journey for fulfillment through a career choice. It is the part of life that I never knew I was missing. Making a website, designing a game, creating a program that provides a convenience for people everywhere is a feeling that provides me peace and I look forward to sharing this passion for years to come.
